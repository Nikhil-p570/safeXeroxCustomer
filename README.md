## 🔒 Stop Leaving Your Data at the Xerox Shop
Have you ever wondered what happens to your files after you send them to a shopkeeper's WhatsApp or Email? They stay there. Forever. 

**Safe Xerox** is designed for the privacy-conscious individual. We believe your personal documents should remain personal, even when you need a physical copy.

---

## 🚀 How to Print Securely in 3 Steps

### 1. Secure Upload
Select your document (PDF, JPG, PNG) in the app. Your file is instantly encrypted on your device. It's safe before it even leaves your hand.

### 2. Scan & Link
Walk into any Safe Xerox partner shop and scan their unique QR code. This creates a secure, temporary handshake between your phone and their printer.

### 3. Print & Self-Destruct
Once the owner hits "Print", the document is sent. As soon as the print job is finished, the digital file **self-destructs** from our servers and the shop's dashboard. No traces left behind.

## 🛠️ Installation for Users

1. **Download the App**: Install the Safe Xerox APK or run from source.
2. **Permissions**: Grant Camera permission (for QR scanning) and Storage permission (to select files).
3. **Start Printing**: No complex setup required. Just open and go.
